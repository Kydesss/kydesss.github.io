Las Tres Maria's Jar Labels

My aunt is an entrepreneur and she is starting a food selling business with my other aunt and my mom. The brand name is Las Tres Marias, since they all have Maria in their name, except for my mom. They asked me to create label/packaging designs for their products such as Gourmet Tuyo, Uga Flakes, Taba Ng Talangka, and Chili Garlic.
